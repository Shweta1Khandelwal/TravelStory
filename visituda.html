City Palace

City Palace, Udaipur, is a palace complex situated in the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan. It was built over a period of nearly 400 years, with contributions from several rulers of the Mewar dynasty.
Address: Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001
Hours: 9:30 am to 5:30 pm
Phone: 0294 241 9021
Architectural style: Rajput Architecture
Architect: Udai Singh II

Monsoon Palace

The Monsoon Palace, also known as the Sajjan Garh Palace, is a hilltop palatial residence in the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan in India, overlooking the Fateh Sagar Lake.
Address: Kodiyat, Rajasthan 313011
Hours: 10 am to 6 pm
Phone: 095710 63039
Function: Palace
Construction started: 1884

Lake Pichola

Lake Pichola, situated in Udaipur city in the Indian state of Rajasthan, is an artificial fresh water lake, created in the year 1362 AD, named after the nearby Picholi village.
Area: 6.96 km²
Catchment area: 55 km²
Max. depth: 8.5 m (28 ft)
Location: Rajasthan
Islands: Jag Mandir, Arsi Vilas, Jag Niwas
Cities: Udaipur

Fateh Sagar Lake

Fateh Sagar Lake is situated in the city of Udaipur in the Indian state of Rajasthan. It is an artificial lake named after Maharana Fateh Singh of Udaipur and Mewar, constructed north-west of Udaipur, to the north of Lake Pichola in the 1680s.
Area: 4 km²
Length: 2.4 km
Location: Udaipur, Rajasthan
Max. depth: 13.4 m (44 ft)
Shore length1: 8.5 km (5.3 mi)
Cities: Udaipur

Jagdish Temple

Jagdish Temple is a large Hindu temple in the middle of Udaipur in Rajasthan. A big tourist attraction, the temple was originally called the temple of Jagannath Rai but is now called Jagdish-ji. It is a major monument in Udaipur.
Address: RJ SH 50, Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001
Phone: 083879 87510
District: Udaipur district

Bohra Ganesh Temple

Bohra Ganesh Temple is a very old and famous temple of Lord Ganesh, situated near Mohanlal Sukhadia University in Udaipur. Having a standing statue of Lord Ganesh at the center, this temple is visited by thousands of devotees every week.
Address: Bohra Ganesh Rd, Ganapati Nagar, Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001

Karni Mata Temple

Shri Manshapurna Karni Mata Temple is a Hindu temple located on the Machla Magra Hills, near the Doodh Talai Lake in Udaipur, Rajasthan. It enshrines the stone idol of Karni Mata.
Address: Jawahar Nagar, Pichola, Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001

Gangaur Ghat

Gangaur Ghat or Gangori Ghat is a main ghat situated near the waterfront of Lake Pichola in Udaipur. It is situated near the Jagdish Chowk area. It is known for Bagore-ki-Haveli, a popular tourist destination of the city.
Address: Pichola, Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001
Hours: Open today · Open 24 hours
Phone: 099064 62202

Ahar Cenotaphs

The Ahar Cenotaphs are a group of royal cenotaphs located in Ahar, Rajasthan, about 2 km east of Udaipur, in Rajasthan, India. The site contains more than 250 cenotaphs of the maharajas of Mewar that were built over approximately 350 years.
Address: Ganapati Nagar, Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001
Hours: 10AM to 5PM
Phone: 0294 247 0004

Udaipur Solar Observatory

The Udaipur Solar Observatory is in Udaipur, Rajasthan in India on an island in the Fateh Sagar Lake. The sky conditions at Udaipur are quite favourable for solar observations.
Address: Near Fatehpur Lake Fatehpur to Badi Talai, Rani Rd, Shilpgram, Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001
Hours: 9:30AM to 5PM
